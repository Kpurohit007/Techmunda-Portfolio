About TechMunda

TechMunda — global, AI‑first technology partner.

TechMunda is a startup dedicated to providing top‑notch technology solutions to clients all over the world. Our mission is to deliver innovative, scalable and reliable tech services while maintaining strong relationships built on trust, loyalty and excellence.

Founded by Keshav Purohit (Founder) and Dhananjay Pathak (Co‑Founder), TechMunda was created to bridge the gap between ideas and execution. With a dedicated team of skilled technology professionals, we ensure every project benefits from cutting‑edge expertise and a client‑first approach.

We serve businesses, startups and individuals globally, offering solutions that combine innovation, efficiency and long‑term value. Whether it’s software development, AI integration, web and mobile apps or IT consulting, TechMunda is committed to helping clients succeed on a global scale.
